#Sigmaid
Emergency button for people who don't have direct access to immediate help. 

##About
Sigmaid is a website, built for people who need urgent help. We created it for people, who live in a less populated city. 
It helps people to get the help they need as fast as possible, even if there isn't any official workers to deliver the service. 

##Features
Emergency button: Calls for help and starts showing your location. 
User registration: Add your name, contacts and diseases
